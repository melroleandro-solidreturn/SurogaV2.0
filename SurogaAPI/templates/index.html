Pre-Registration

With the Suroga API, you can pre-register rides worldwide using our APP. After the pre-registration, we start searching for your surrogate on the fly. When you book the ride a surrogate will be available at the selected place.

Booking

The booking process consists of defining the date and time for a ride using the Suroga API. We have a worldwide network of partners committed to finding the right surrogates. We enshore a person to do a video stream fuffiling your requisites.

Streaming

We selected Microsoft as our video stream provider. The stream of your rides is deployed using Microsoft teams. Every time a ride is booked we provide a virtual room where you or your clients can meet with the selected surrogate.

Monitoring

Every APP registered with our API has a dashboard. Here you can find your API Key and API calls statistics. In this dashboard, you have also access to your billing board.
